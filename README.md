# 🚴 Bike Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Bike Sales Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel.
It analyzes customer demographics and behavioral factors to understand **what drives bike purchases**.

The dashboard is designed to provide quick insights using filters, KPIs, and visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Identify key factors influencing bike purchases
* Build an interactive and user-friendly Excel dashboard
* Practice data cleaning, pivot tables, and visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔝 KPI Metrics

* Total Customers
* Bikes Purchased (Count & %)
* Average Income
* Conversion Rate

---

### 🎛️ Filters (Slicers)

* Marital Status
* Region
* Education
* Gender *(optional)*
* Age Bracket *(optional)*

---

### 📈 Visualizations

#### 1. Avg Income per Purchase

* Compares income levels between buyers and non-buyers
* Segmented by gender

#### 2. Customer Commute Distance

* Shows how commute distance affects purchase decisions

#### 3. Customer Age Brackets

* Identifies which age groups are more likely to purchase bikes

#### 4. Additional Insights (Optional)

* Region-wise purchase trends
* Education level vs purchase behavior
* Marital status vs purchase

## 💡 Key Insights

* Customers with higher income are more likely to purchase bikes
* Shorter commute distances show higher purchase rates
* Certain age groups (e.g., middle-aged customers) dominate purchases
* Demographics like education and region influence buying behavior
